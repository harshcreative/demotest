Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, any individual with the following income types can use TaxManager services:

  • Income from Salary
  • Income from House Property
  • Income from sale of saving schemes like Shares, Mutual Funds, Bonds
  • Income from Other Sources — interest, dividends, lottery winnings, horse race winnings, etc.
  • Register with TaxManager.in and fill in your Personal Information.
  • Enter financial details — income sources, investments, expenditure, losses, etc.
  • TaxManager.in generates your return based on this data.
  • View your tax computation and review all information.
  • Make a payment based on the Filing Plan you chose.
  • Authorize TaxManager.in to e-file your Income Tax Return.
  • Receive an Acknowledgement via email (if return is signed digitally).
  • Go to your My Account page.
  • Click on Create New ITR and give it an appropriate name.
  • Click the Submit button — your ITR will appear in the My Account page.
  • Click the Edit button to insert and update your information.
  • After creating your ITR, click the Edit button to begin entering information.
  • Follow the Personal Information link to fill in your personal details.
  • Follow the Salary link for income sources and other financial details.
  • Follow the Deduction link for investments and expenditures.
  • Complete remaining sections — Losses, Tax Computation, Bank Details, Filing Status, AIR Details, and Verification.
  • Follow the Income link on My ITR Page.
  • Click Edit on the Income Summary page to provide income source details.
  • Income from Salary: Click Edit to provide employer details from your Form-16, then fill salary and deduction information. Repeat for each Form-16.
    1. Click Edit and enter your Employer's details as shown in Form-16.
    2. Click Save & Continue with Form-16.
    3. Fill income details and investment/deduction information.
    4. Click Save and Continue. Repeat for each employer.
  • Income from House Property: Enter rental income or housing loan interest from your Form-16.
  • Income from Capital Gains: Enter income from Mutual Funds, Shares, Bonds, and other capital assets.
  • Income from Other Sources: Enter income from family pension, agriculture, rent, lottery, gifts, etc.
  • Clubbing of Income: Provide details if you wish to combine income with a family member.
