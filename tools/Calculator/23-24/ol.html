Union Budget Highlights 2023

On 1 Feb 2023 , our honourable Finance minister Nirmala Sitharaman presented her last full year budget of the Modi government. Major highlights for a taxpayer from the presentation are listed below-

Major Highlights Of The Budget 2023

  • The 75th year of Independence has recognised India as a bright star.
  • The new slab rate for the Fy 2023-24 is as follows
  • Annual Income Rate Of Tax
    0 to 3,00,000 Nil
    3,00,000 to 6,00,000 5%
    6,00,000 to 9,00,000 10%
    9,00,000 to 12,00,000 15%
    12,00,000 to 15,00,000 20%
    Above 15,00,000 30%
  • The Government has enhanced income tax exemption limit up to Rs. 7 lakh under the new tax regime
  • Individuals with an annual income of Rs.9 lakh will only pay tax of Rs. 45 thousand only earlier it was Rs. 60 thousand.
  • Standard deduction under the New Tax regime for individuals with income up to Rs.15 lakh will be Rs. 52,400.
  • The tax exemption limit is Rs.25 lakh on leave encashment on retirement for non-government salaried employees.

Is It Important To Consult A Tax Professional?

We are living in an era of professionals, there are experts in every field to assist and guide a layman. For a taxpayer, filing taxes can be confusing and if left as a last-minute job it will be frustrating also. At the onset of the financial budget every year there are changes brought about in the Income-tax slab rates and the rules and regulations of the Income-tax act. The dilemma to hire a professional and file your tax return or if you have complete knowledge and are capable of filing an error-free tax return then do it yourself will prevail till the time you understand the benefits of hiring an expert.

Why A Tax Expert Is Required?

Filing an Income Tax return may be a simple procedure for an individual who has complete knowledge about taxation laws but there are updations time and again which one has to keep in touch with although for some, there are possibilities of complex finances and many sources of income than professional assistance is a must.

  • To file an error-free tax return - There are minimal chances of mistakes if an expert helps an individual to file a tax return. As they have complete knowledge of the procedure you can remain calm and relaxed.
  • Complex returns- For some taxpayers, there are various sources of income and foreign assets. In a year there could be many transactions which require professional assistance at the time of filing the tax return.
  • Updation In The Income Tax Act- There are many changes in the Income Tax Act for the FY 2022-23 from adding Crypto tax, taxes on long-term capital gains, filing of updated income tax returns and many more which will require expert hands to complete your filing of tax returns for smooth sailing.
  • Not Expensive- If an individual chooses peace over panic then the cost paid to hire a professional would seem minimal. These experts charge a very nominal amount to give you a hassle-free experience for filing Income Tax Returns.
  • Saving And Investment Plans- If you file your taxes correctly then you can save money for future economic growth. As your tax returns are filed correctly and efficiently, a taxpayer will save money which can be further used in tax-saving investments to gain deductions and avail tax-saving options.

Conclusion- Ask For Help

An individual sometimes keeps pushing important tasks like filing Income Tax returns furthermore as the date to file the return is extended. But all this procrastination of tasks will lead to hassles and wrong filing of the returns. Therefore to file an error-free tax return it is beneficial to ask for professional help
